## Projects

### 1. Sales Performance Dashboard
**File:** Sales_Dashboard_Project.xlsx
**Built on:** Day 4 of 90-day journey

**What's inside:**
- 20 sales transactions across 4 regions and 5 products
- 3 Pivot Tables — Category, Region, Salesperson analysis
- Interactive Dashboard with Pie, Bar and Column charts
- KPI Summary — Total Revenue, Top Region, Top Salesperson

**Key Insights:**
- Electronics drives 84% of total revenue
- South region is the top performing territory
- Priya is the top salesperson at ₹9,12,000
